Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì How do I use the AI chat feature?

Simply open the chat in the app and type your question about your pet's health. The AI assistant will provide general information and advice. Remember, this is not a substitute for professional veterinary care.

üì∏ How do I analyze a photo of my pet?

Go to the "Photo Analysis" section, select or take a photo of your pet, and tap "Analyze". The AI will analyze the photo and provide insights. For accurate diagnosis, always consult a licensed veterinarian.

üíä How do I add medications and set reminders?

Navigate to your pet's profile, go to "Medications", and tap the "+" button to add a medication. You can set reminders for medication times, and the app will send you notifications.

üìÖ How do I schedule appointments and vaccinations?

In your pet's profile, you'll find sections for "Appointments" and "Vaccinations". Add new entries and set dates. The app will remind you of upcoming appointments and vaccinations.

üåç Is the app available in my language?

PetWellness AI supports 15 languages: English, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Polish, Russian, Turkish, Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Arabic, and Hindi. The app automatically detects your device language.

üîí How is my data protected?

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your data on our servers. When using AI features, your messages and photos are processed by third-party AI services (Claude, Google Gemini) according to their privacy policies. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
